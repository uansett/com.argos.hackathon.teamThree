Product description / spec

pdp_proddesc2colleft

The LG 60LA620V Smart 3D TV offers an immersive viewing experience. With LG Smart TV you can watch catch up TV, movies on demand, TV series and listen to music instantly. LG's CINEMA 3Dtechnology offers flicker free 3D viewing and also features built-in Wi-Fi, Freeview HD and comes with 4 pairs of comfortable 3D glasses.

Top 5 Smart apps include: LOVEFiLM, BBC iPlayer, Netflix, blinkbox, Acetrax.

Television picture quality:

  • Direct-lit LED TV Screen.
  • Smart TV.
  • Passive 3D TV with optional 2D to 3D conversion.
  • Includes 4 pairs of 3D glasses.
  • Screen size: 60in (152.4cm).
  • Resolution 1920 x 1080 pixels.
  • Motion rate 200Hz.
  • Viewing angle 178/178 degrees.

Connectivity:

  • 1 SCART socket, 3 USB ports and 3 HDMI sockets.
  • Component video socket.
  • Composite.
  • Optical connection.
  • Headphone socket.
  • AV socket.
  • Wi-Fi enabled.
  • Ethernet connection.
  • DLNA compatibility.
  • Mobile high-definition link.
  • Miracast.
  • Open browser.

Sound quality:

  • Virtual Surround sound system.
  • 2 x 10 watts RMS power output.

Digital features:

  • Freeview HD digital tuner.
  • Saorview digital tuner (Saorview can only be received in the Republic of Ireland).
  • 8 day electronic programme guide (EPG).
  • CI+ slot.

Additional features:

  • Sleep timer.
  • Child lock.
  • Size of TV H78.3, W135.5, D6.5cm.
  • Weight of TV 23.1kg (unpackaged).
  • Size of TV with stand: H66.5, W135.5, D34.3cm.
  • Weight of TV with stand: 25.9kg.
  • Suitable for wall mounting 400 x 400 bracket.

Energy efficiency information:

  • Energy efficiency rating: A+.
  • Energy efficiency index (EEI) 0.2.
  • On mode power consumption 98 watts.
  • Annual power consumption 143kWh.
  • Standby power consumption 0.4 watt.
  • This product contains lead.
  • Mercury content 0mg.

EAN: 8806084179944
